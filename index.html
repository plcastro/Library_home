About ACLC Library

The Library provides a comprehensive collection of books, journals, and other resources related to information technology and computer science. The library serves as a learning resource for students enrolled in the AMA Computer Learning Center and as a resource for the wider community of professionals and researchers in the field of information technology. The library may offer a range of services and facilities, such as access to online databases, a quiet study area, and computers with internet access. It may also offer training and support to help users get the most out of its resources and technology. The ACLC Library is a valuable resource for anyone seeking information and knowledge in the field of information technology and computer science.
